Prophylactic Support System

Our neuroscientists have spent decades developing incredible techniques to preserve the soul once the body approaches death. This procedure is painless and takes less than two minutes.

Through lengthy testing, our medical professionals are able to determine the precise week of a patients’ death, if not the day and hour. To avoid inducing our patients into a state of crisis, we keep this information private; there are few exceptions.
